About Us

ABOUT PROJECT

Our mission is to raise awareness and educate Filipino non-Muslims about the Islam culture, because not only does false portrayal affect them personally, but it also has a negative effect to the Islam. We aim to provide factual and reliable information about our Muslim brothers and sisters to further understand and accept them as people and as a religion. We believe that Islam is not a religion of extremism and they are not what most people think they are that is why A Peace of Islam is something that would help change the perspective and enlighten non-Muslims towards Muslims.
