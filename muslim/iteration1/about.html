About us

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Our mission is to raise awareness and educate Filipino non-Muslims about the Islam culture, because not only does false portrayal affect them personally, but it also has a negative effect to the Islam. We aim to provide factual and reliable information about our Muslim brothers and sisters to further understand and accept them as people and as a religion. We believe that Islam is not a religion of extremism and they are not what most people think they are that is why A Peace of Islam is something that would help change the perspective and enlighten non-Muslims towards Muslims.

THE CREATORS

The creators are both Multimedia Arts students from De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde. Anne Soriano likes to present her art through different platforms such as videography and photography, while Micaella Lipana is a graphic and website designer as well as an illustrator. They both want to change the way people think and how media portrays the Muslims through presenting the effects of the negative representation as well as reliable facts about the Islam culture; with the help of the people who also wants change and awareness, the creators will correct the media through media.
