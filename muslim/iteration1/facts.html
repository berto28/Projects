FACTS

Hijab

Muslim women wear the head-covering (hijab) in fulfillment of God's decree to dress modestly.

Islamic prayer

For Muslims, the five daily prayer times (called salat) are among the most important obligations of the Islamic faith. Prayers remind the faithful of God and the many opportunities to seek His guidance and forgiveness.

Prayer Mat

Islamic prayer mats, or rugs, are individual-sized mats used by many Muslims in daily prayer rituals. Islam lectures unity, so there are no seats or held spots inside the mosque.

Halal

It is an Arabic word meaning lawful or permitted. In reference to food, it is the dietary standard, as prescribed in the Qur'an. Muslims are only allowed to eat meat that has been prepared according to Islamic law.

Muslims does not eat pork

The main reason pork is forbidden for Muslims is because it says in the Holy Quran that some food is allowed, while others are explicitly declared haram, which means forbidden. And pork is one of those forbidden foods.
