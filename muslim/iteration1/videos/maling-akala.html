Maling Akala

Jamila's family had to move from Mindanao to Manila because of her father's job and with the hopes of having better lives. Manila, being a Catholic/Christian majority city, what she thought would change her life positively didn't really go with what she was expecting. Maling Akala is based on true events from people who are affected by such bullying and discrimination.
